IRC only / How it works

At the top of every hour, Botto picks a winner.

The channel bot picks a random nick from #TheZone on IRC and kicks them. Most people have auto-rejoin enabled, so they pop right back in. Probably.

The kicks have been logged since 2008, and it turned into a scoreboard. People want to win because every kick increases their kickstats.

Wednesday protocol

Caps Lock Wednesday

Every Wednesday from 5-6pm ET, everyone changes their nick to a capital version and talks in all caps only. The capslock key gets some exercise.

CLW

It is loud, overengineered, and exactly the point.

Most regulars script the whole thing: nick changes, automatic text transforms, the works. For an hour, the channel turns into a very committed bit, and somehow everyone understands the assignment.

IRC client required

Opening IRC from the browser

IRC links only work if you have an IRC client installed and your browser is allowed to hand off irc:// or ircs:// links to it.

If nothing opens or you receive an error, check your browser protocol-handler settings, or open your IRC client manually and connect to irc.tzirc.com / #TheZone.
